About C.O.L.E

C.O.L.E was developed buy 4 students Houston sprouse, Taylor Rogers, Kory Kinter, and Aaron Burgin. C.O.L.E was made to help people all around the world see how different countries compare to eachother in their quality of life scores and cost of living scores in one easy to use place. We hope this website is able to help some find and live a better life! All data was provided by kaggle.com and the data can be found at https://www.kaggle.com/cityapiio/countries-quality-of-life-index-2020-year and https://www.kaggle.com/andradaolteanu/2020-cost-of-living please give them credit for their work.
